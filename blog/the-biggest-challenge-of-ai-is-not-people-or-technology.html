The biggest challenge of AI is not people or technology

May 2, 2018

Yes, that's correct. Well, I think so and let me explain. I think the biggest challenge of AI right now is making it an industry or an engineering field. All the other challenges were there for a while if you really think about it. There can be slow downs and booms in fundamental research, talent can be in short supply, still we will make progress. But I think we are yet to define AI as an industry. And we need to do it right away! But why..? We all can see how AI is changing the world; from photos we take to how we move around. It is becoming more common and ubiquitous everyday. From a technical point of view this means the systems we use in our day-to-day life have much more AI underneath.
